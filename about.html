About Me

The artist behind the pixels

I'm a 3D artist with expertise in modeling, texturing, and product design.

I create high-quality assets for both stylized and realistic visuals, tailored for commercial, digital, and product visualization projects.

With over 5 years of professional experience, I've worked with clients across various industries to bring their concepts to life in three dimensions. My passion lies in the intersection of art and technology, where I can push the boundaries of what's possible in digital creation.

When I'm not crafting 3D models, you can find me experimenting with new rendering techniques, studying real-world materials, or mentoring aspiring 3D artists.
