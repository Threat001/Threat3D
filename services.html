My Process

How I approach each project

1

Consultation

We discuss your project requirements, goals, and vision to ensure I understand exactly what you need.

2

Concept & Blockout

I create initial concepts and rough 3D blockouts to establish the basic forms and composition.

3

Modeling & Detailing

The model is refined with proper topology and all necessary details are added.

4

Texturing & Materials

Materials and textures are created to bring the model to life with realistic or stylized surfaces.

5

Lighting & Rendering

The scene is lit and rendered to showcase the model in its best possible presentation.

6

Delivery & Feedback

Final files are delivered in your required formats, with revisions based on your feedback.
